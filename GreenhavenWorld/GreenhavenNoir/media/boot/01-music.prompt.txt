TITLE MUSIC PROMPT — Greenhaven: Noir Title Theme

A cinematic title theme for a dark noir interactive novel. Begins with the sound of rain — 10 seconds of pure atmosphere. Then a low cello enters with a slow, descending melody — melancholic but dignified. Joined by a deep synth pad and the distant, processed sound of a copper bell. The mood: this is the overture to a city that is alive, ancient, and watching. Not horror. Not sadness. Presence. Tempo: 55-60 BPM. Key: D minor. Duration: 2-3 minutes. Should loop seamlessly for the title/menu screen. Instruments: cello, deep synth pads, distant bell, processed rain, subtle metallic textures. The track should feel like standing at the edge of the city at midnight, rain on your face, looking up at a million dark windows — and knowing one of them just flickered.
