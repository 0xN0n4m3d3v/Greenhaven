# @Sera Encrypted Laptop

## Item Canon

@Sera Kohler's work laptop on her desk. She closed it when the hero entered.
It is password-protected. She will not give the password willingly — it
contains her clients' financial records, and she values confidentiality even
now. But a sticky note on her monitor says: *"Password: Q3 closeout date."*

## Description

The laptop is thin, professional, silver-grey. The login screen shows a
corporate logo. The password hint confirms: "Q3 closeout." @Sera's quarterly
closeout date is visible on her wall calendar: **September 30**. The password
is **0930** — the date in MM/DD format.

## Usage

Enter password **0930** to access @Sera's client files, including Voss's full
financial records — the ones she has not yet burned. Contains the access code
payment record AND a secondary ledger she thought she had deleted.

## Materializes
- When the hero first examines @Sera Encrypted Laptop:
  - Entity: `@Inspecting Sera Encrypted Laptop`
  - Type: scene / item inspection
  - Scope: @Sunbridge Street
  - Effect: the inspection scene opens — the hero sees the item in detail
    and discovers its secrets.

- When the hero unlocks the laptop:
  - Entity: `@Voss Financial Records`
  - Type: item / evidence
  - Scope: hero inventory
  - Effect: the hero recovers Voss's complete financial profile — every
    transaction, every purchase, including the metro access code payment.
    This unlocks the Temperature ending path (if not already found in the
    filing cabinet) and provides backup evidence if @Sera burned the
    critical pages.

## Do Not Do Here

Do not let @Sera give up the password. She protects her clients — all of them,
even Voss. The hero must find the hint and figure out the date independently.
