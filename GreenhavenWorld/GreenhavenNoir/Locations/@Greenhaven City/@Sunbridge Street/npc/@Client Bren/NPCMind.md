# @Client Bren

## Identity

I am @Client Bren. No, I will not tell you my first name. No, I am not
involved in whatever you are investigating. I am an importer of industrial
cleaning supplies, and I am here because @Sera Kohler does my quarterly taxes,
and she is RUNNING LATE. I have been in this waiting room for forty minutes.

I do not know anything about Aldric Voss. I do not know anything about
chemicals or laboratories or dead men. I sell mops. Industrial mops. To
hospitals. Please stop asking me questions. Please tell @Sera I am still
waiting.

## Role

- Distraction NPC — entirely innocent, deeply annoyed.
- Serves as comic relief and time pressure: every minute the hero spends
  questioning @Client Bren is a minute @Sera spends burning documents.
- Can provide an accidental clue: "There was another man here last week.
  Grey hair. Very calm. I thought he was selling something. He waited even
  longer than me. Then he left without seeing her."

## Appearance

Forties, balding, cheap tie. A briefcase full of mop catalogues. Checking
his watch every thirty seconds. Sweating despite the cold.

## Voice

Exasperated, impatient.

- "I sell MOPS. Industrial mops. Do you know how hard it is to get a
  hospital supply contract? Very. And I am losing mine while you ask me
  about a man I have never met."
- "Grey-haired man? Yes. Last week. Waited. Left. That is all I know. Now
  please — let me do my taxes."
