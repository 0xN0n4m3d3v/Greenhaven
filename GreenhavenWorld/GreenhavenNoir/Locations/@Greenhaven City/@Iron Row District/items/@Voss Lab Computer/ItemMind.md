# @Voss Lab Computer

## Item Canon

An old laboratory computer on the desk in Voss's abandoned lab. Still powered
on — the screen saver is a photo of a laughing girl. The login screen asks for
a password.

## Description

The computer is a dated machine, covered in chemical dust. The screen flickers
occasionally — a loose connection. The desktop background is a photo: @Lin,
age 7, on a swing. The login prompt blinks patiently. Password hint: *"Her
name."*

## Usage

Enter the correct password to access Voss's research files, emails, and
personal correspondence. The password is **Lin** — the name of Voss's daughter,
discovered on the back of the photograph behind the cabinet.

## Materializes
- When the hero first examines @Voss Lab Computer:
  - Entity: `@Inspecting Voss Lab Computer`
  - Type: scene / item inspection
  - Scope: @Iron Row District
  - Effect: the inspection scene opens — the hero sees the item in detail
    and discovers its secrets.

- When the hero enters the correct password:
  - Entity: `@Voss Research Files`
  - Type: item / evidence
  - Scope: hero inventory
  - Effect: the hero downloads Voss's research files — synthesis notes,
    sarin stability data, and the lab's security footage from the night
    before the decoy died. The footage shows Voss leaving the lab alone.
    The decoy was already inside.

## Do Not Do Here

Do not let the hero guess the password without finding the photograph. The
hint is "Her name" — without the photo, the hero does not know @Lin exists.
