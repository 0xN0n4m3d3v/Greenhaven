PORTRAIT PROMPT — Old Mirek

An elderly bartender, seventy, massive, bald. Hands like hams, polishing the same glass over and over. A stained apron over a shirt that was white thirty years ago. One eye clouded with an untreated cataract. He never looks at you directly — he looks at the glass. Background: a nameless bar in an industrial district, dark wood, empty stools, a single light above the counter. Style: warm but worn noir portrait, the polished glass catching the only light.
