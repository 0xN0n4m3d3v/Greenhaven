IMAGE PROMPT — Metro Central Hub Establishing Shot

A vast underground cathedral of concrete and pipework — the central ventilation hub of the metro system. Six levels of catwalks rise into shadow. Pipes thick as barrels run along every surface. At the centre, on the lowest catwalk: a gaunt man in a long dark wool coat (@The Chemist), grey hair, standing beside a master control panel. One hand rests inside his right coat pocket — where the remote trigger waits. Emergency lights pulse green. Condensation drips from above. A small screen on the panel shows six armed canister indicators. The air hums — the city's breath, concentrated here. Style: monumental industrial noir, vertiginous scale, the lone figure dwarfed by machinery, green light against infinite shadow. Square 1:1, 2048x2048.
