# @Emergency Exit Hatch

## Item Canon

A heavy metal hatch on the upper catwalk level of the Central Hub — the
emergency exit to the surface. It is sealed with an electronic keypad. Voss
locked it from the inside to prevent anyone from escaping the hub during the
attack. The code is the one number Voss could never forget: @Lin's birthday.

## Description

The hatch is industrial-grade — designed to survive a metro fire. The keypad
blinks red: LOCKED. A small label beneath the keypad reads: "Emergency
override — enter employee ID." Voss reprogrammed it. His employee ID was
deactivated when he left the Industrial Complex. But @Dorn's ID still works —
**DORN-8824** — because no one thought to revoke it after he fled. Pallas
tells the hero this, if asked.

## Usage

Enter **DORN-8824** to unlock the emergency exit. This opens a direct route
to the surface — an escape path for the hero, or for civilians if the
temperature/evacuation path is used. It also provides a second entry point
to the hub for the Sunday confrontation (the hero can enter from above,
within jammer range of Voss).

## Materializes
- When the hero first examines @Emergency Exit Hatch:
  - Entity: `@Inspecting Emergency Exit Hatch`
  - Type: scene / item inspection
  - Scope: @Metro Central Hub
  - Effect: the inspection scene opens — the hero sees the item in detail
    and discovers its secrets.

- When the hero unlocks the emergency hatch:
  - Entity: `@Surface Access Route`
  - Type: access / escape route
  - Scope: `@Metro Central Hub`
  - Effect: the hero now has an escape route AND an alternative entry point.
    On Sunday, the hero can choose to enter through the service tunnels
    (@Dorn's key) or through the emergency hatch from the surface. Two
    approaches, two tactical options.

## Do Not Do Here

Do not let the hero guess @Dorn's ID. The code must come from @Chief Engineer
Pallas or from @Dorn himself.
