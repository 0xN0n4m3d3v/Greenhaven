IMAGE PROMPT — Ancient Loop Scene

Extreme close-up of a wet sidewalk. Raindrops hitting the black asphalt in frozen concentric circles — each ripple a perfect ring. In the reflection of the largest puddle: a flickering neon sign, an empty road stretching to infinity, and a silhouetted human figure lying motionless. The reflection forms a perfect circle, suggesting an endless loop. Beyond the puddle: absolute darkness. Style: abstract noir photography, extreme shallow depth of field, almost monochromatic black and grey with a single thread of neon green cutting through the water reflection. Square 1:1 composition, 2048x2048.
