IMAGE PROMPT — Midnight Bell Scene

Extreme low-angle view from the base of a crumbling concrete tower, looking straight up. The tower fills most of the frame — its surface cracked, streaked with rust, water running down in dark rivulets. A single searchlight rotates slowly near the top, its beam cutting through rain and fog. At the very peak, a massive dark copper bell — its surface wet and glistening, its heavy iron tongue visible in the moment of striking. Rain falls directly toward the camera lens. The sky behind the tower is absolute black except for one razor-thin line of crimson at the distant horizon. Style: extreme low-angle noir photography, heavy chiaroscuro, the bell as the single dominant focal point. Dramatic, monumental, inhuman. Square 1:1 composition, 2048x2048.
