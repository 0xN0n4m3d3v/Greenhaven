IMAGE PROMPT — Cold Design Scene

A long, empty road stretching into darkness. A single flickering neon sign on a metal pole — its light catching the rain in frozen pulses, glowing a sickly green-orange. The asphalt is wet, perfectly reflective, mirroring the sign's broken glow in long streaks. No people. No vehicles. Just the road, the sign, the rain, and the infinite dark beyond. Style: film noir photography, high contrast black and white with a single color accent — the dying neon. The sign centered at the vanishing point. Square 1:1 composition, 2048x2048.
