IMAGE PROMPT — Crimson Dusk Scene

A skyline of crumbling concrete towers silhouetted against a dying sun — the sky bleeding from deep orange to surgical red to the bruised purple of night. The horizon line is a wound. In the foreground, a street gutter overflowing with water that has a distinctly red tint, reflecting the bleeding sky. Steam rises from an iron grate. Two dark shadows on a nearby concrete wall are bent at unnatural angles that do not match any visible light source. Style: dark painterly noir, heavy on the reds and deep purples, wet surfaces throughout, chiaroscuro. Square 1:1 composition, 2048x2048. No text, no visible people — only their wrong shadows.
