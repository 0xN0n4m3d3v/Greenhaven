IMAGE PROMPT — Greenhaven City Establishing Shot

A noir cityscape at night from a low angle, looking up. Wet black asphalt in the foreground reflecting neon light — a flickering sign with unreadable glyphs casting green and orange onto the rain. Mid-ground: empty street, a single sodium lamp, shadows that stretch wrong. Background: a skyline of crumbling concrete towers with hollow, unlit windows — hundreds of them. At the highest tower, a dark copper bell visible against a bleeding crimson sky. Cold rain streaks through every layer. Style: dark painterly noir, heavy chiaroscuro, muted palette except for neon and the crimson horizon line. No text, no UI, no people visible. Square 1:1 composition, 2048x2048.
