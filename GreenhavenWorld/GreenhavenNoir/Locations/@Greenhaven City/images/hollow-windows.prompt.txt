IMAGE PROMPT — Hollow Windows Scene

View looking up at a skyline of abandoned high-rise buildings at night. Hundreds of dark windows arranged in a brutalist grid — most of them completely black, a few visibly shattered, and exactly ONE single window in the middle distance glowing with a faint, impossible warm light. Cold rain streaks diagonally down the entire frame. Steam rises from a street grate in the lower third of the image, catching the orange glow of a sodium lamp from off-frame left. The perspective is from street level looking up — the hero's point of view. The glowing window feels like an eye that has just opened. Style: atmospheric noir photography, deep shadows, a single point of warmth in a sea of cold grey and black, strong vertical lines. Square 1:1 composition, 2048x2048. No text, no people.
