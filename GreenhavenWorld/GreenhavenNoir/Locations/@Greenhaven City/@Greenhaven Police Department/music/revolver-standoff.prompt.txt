MUSIC PROMPT — Revolver Standoff

A tense, percussive cue for armed confrontation in the metro tunnels and central hub. Begins with silence and the distant hum of ventilation. Then: a heartbeat-like bass pulse — slow, deliberate. A single deep cello note, bowed with increasing pressure. The sound of a revolver cylinder rotating — a metallic click, cold and clean. Silence. Then the heartbeat resumes, faster now. This is not an action theme — it is a TENSION theme. The space between heartbeats is where the choice lives. Tempo: the pulse IS the tempo — one beat every 2 seconds, accelerating to every 1 second. Key: D minor with dissonant undertones. Instruments: processed heartbeat/sub-bass pulse, cello (bowed with extreme pressure), revolver cylinder click (sampled metal), ventilation hum, silence. Duration: 2-3 minutes. NOT looped — scene cue. The track should feel like the moment between drawing a weapon and deciding to fire.
