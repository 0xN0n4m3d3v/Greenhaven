MUSIC PROMPT — Loop Broken Cue

A quiet, resolved cue for the ending of the loop. A simple, gentle piano melody — four or five notes, repeated with slight variation, like someone taking a breath after holding it for a very long time. Underneath: the softest possible ambient drone — the city, still present, but no longer pressing. No rain. No clock. No count. The feeling of Monday morning without a reset. Tempo: very slow, free time, 50 BPM. Key: C major (for the first and only time in the cartridge — a subtle shift from the minor keys that dominate the soundtrack). Instruments: felt piano (close-mic'd, intimate), soft cello sustain, the barest whisper of city ambience without rain. Duration: 2-3 minutes, can loop but is intended as a scene coda. The track should feel like exhaling.
