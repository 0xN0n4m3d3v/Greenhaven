MUSIC PROMPT — Monday Morning: The Loop Resets

A short, self-contained cue for the loop reset scene. Begins with the buzz of a fluorescent light flickering — once, twice, three times — before catching. Then a single low piano note, held and left to decay — the sound of waking up and remembering that you died. A brief moment of dissonance (a processed metallic scrape — the echo of the death that just happened) that fades into the steady institutional drone of the department. The mood: disorientation resolving into grim determination. NOT looped — this is a scene cue, played once per reset. Tempo: free time, very slow. Key: C minor with a brief dissonant cluster. Instruments: processed fluorescent buzz, felt piano (one note, long decay), metallic scrape, low cello drone. Duration: 90 seconds to 2 minutes. The track should feel like opening your eyes and realising: Monday. Again.
