MUSIC PROMPT — Police Department Ambient (Monday 6AM)

A tired, institutional ambient piece for a run-down police department at the start of an endless week. The low buzz of fluorescent lights as a drone — steady, slightly out of tune. A slow, weary piano melody — simple, four or five notes, repeating with slight variation, like someone going through the same morning ritual for the hundredth time. In the background: the distant clatter of a teletype, the squeak of a desk chair, the crackle of a police radio too far away to understand. The mood: not sad — exhausted. The sound of a place that has seen too many Mondays. Tempo: very slow, 45-55 BPM. Key: C minor. Instruments: felt piano (close-mic'd, intimate), processed fluorescent hum as pad, distant teletype and radio static, silence. Duration: 3-4 minutes, seamless loop. The track should feel like 6:00 AM in a room with no windows.
