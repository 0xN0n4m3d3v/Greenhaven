MUSIC PROMPT — Sunday Gas Attack Cue

A short, devastating cue for the opening scene of the game. Begins with the ambient hum of a metro carriage — distant wheels, the subtle rattle of fittings, a faint electrical buzz. Then: a slow, creeping high-frequency tone — barely audible at first, growing like gas filling a room. The sound of something sweet and lethal. A single cello note bowed with increasing pressure — not melody, but TENSION. The moment of the hero's first death should feel like a held breath that is never released. Ends abruptly — a cut to silence, then the buzz of a fluorescent light flickering (the transition to Monday morning). NOT looped — this is the opening cue. Duration: 90-120 seconds. Key: atonal with a resolving drone to C minor. Instruments: processed metro ambience, high-frequency sine wave, cello (bowed with extreme pressure), silence, fluorescent buzz.
