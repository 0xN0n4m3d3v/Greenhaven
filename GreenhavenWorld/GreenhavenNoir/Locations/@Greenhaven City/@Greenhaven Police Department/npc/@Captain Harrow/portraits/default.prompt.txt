PORTRAIT PROMPT — Captain Harrow

A police captain in his fifties, heavy build, cheap grey suit dry-cleaned too many times. Grey hair cropped short — military style, though he never served. Reading glasses on a chain around his neck. A coffee mug visible on the desk before him: "WORLD'S OKAYEST CAPTAIN." His desk is immaculate — not from organisation, but from doing no actual police work. His hands are soft. His eyes are hard — the eyes of a bureaucrat who has survived three mayors and one buried internal affairs investigation. Background: a precinct office, filing cabinets, a window with rain streaking down. Style: institutional noir portrait, harsh overhead fluorescent, desaturated. Square 1:1, 2048x2048. No text except what naturally appears on the mug.
