SCENE IMAGE PROMPT — Trapped Forever

The coffee cup beside them is cold, a skin of mould on the surface. The case board through the doorway: empty, cobwebbed. The mirror shows nothing — just a grey haze where a reflection should be. In the locker room doorway: a hooded figure — @The Hobo — turning away, one grey hand on the doorframe, about to leave. The fluorescent lights no longer flicker. They barely glow. Style: haunting still-life noir, the stillness of surrender. Square 1:1, 2048x2048. No visible faces. The atmosphere of an ending where no one wins.
