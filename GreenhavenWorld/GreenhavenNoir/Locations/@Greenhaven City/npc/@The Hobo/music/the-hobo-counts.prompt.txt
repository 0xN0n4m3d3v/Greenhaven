MUSIC PROMPT — The Hobo Counts Theme

A variation of the Hobo theme for the counting scene. The familiar trash-can fire crackle and rain. But now: a soft, regular pulse — like a heartbeat or a clock — underlying everything. A single cello note, plucked (pizzicato), repeating at a slow, steady interval — one, two, three... the rhythm of counting. Between counts: silence and rain. The mood: the weight of numbers. Each pluck is a death. Each silence is a week lived and lost. NOT looped — this is a scene cue. Tempo: the pulse IS the tempo — one count every 3-4 seconds. Key: F minor. Instruments: pizzicato cello (repeating single note), fire crackle, rain, soft heartbeat-like sub-bass pulse, silence. Duration: 2-3 minutes. The track should feel like sitting beside someone who is keeping a ledger of your deaths — and turning the page.
