MUSIC PROMPT — The Hobo Speaks Theme

A slightly more present variation of the Hobo theme — for the rare moments when the figure actually speaks. Begins with the familiar trash-can fire crackle and rain. Then a single deep piano note — struck clearly, held, and left to decay. After the decay: a low cello enters with a two-note motif — simple, deliberate, like words being chosen carefully. The feeling of someone breaking a long silence to say something that matters. NOT looped — this track plays once per scene trigger. Tempo: free time, very slow. Key: F minor. Instruments: felt piano (single notes with long decay), close-mic'd cello, fire, rain, silence. Duration: 2-3 minutes. The track should feel like the moment between someone opening their mouth and the first word coming out.
