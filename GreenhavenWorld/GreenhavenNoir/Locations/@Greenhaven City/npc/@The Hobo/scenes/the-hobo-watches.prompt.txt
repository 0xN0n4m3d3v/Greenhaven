SCENE IMAGE PROMPT — The Hobo Watches

A narrow wet alley at night. A trash-can fire burning in the centre — flames steady and unnaturally even, as if they are not consuming fuel but simply existing. On an overturned wooden crate beside the fire: a hooded figure wrapped in layers of faded coats and scarves, face entirely lost in shadow. One grey hand extended toward the fire — palm up, catching warmth or offering it. An empty space on the crate — clearly an invitation for someone to sit. Rain falling in streaks everywhere except the small dry zone around the fire. Brick walls streaked with rust and the ghosts of old graffiti. Steam rising from a nearby grate. Style: noir painterly, the fire as the single warm light in cold darkness, deep chiaroscuro, the atmosphere of a shared silence. Square 1:1, 2048x2048. No text. No visible face.
