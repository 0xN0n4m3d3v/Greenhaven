# @Greenhaven Industrial Complex

@Greenhaven Industrial Complex is where the city's chemistry happens — or used
to happen, before half the plants shut down. Chemical Storage Unit 7 is a
windowless concrete block at the edge of the complex, surrounded by chain-link
fence and sodium lights that turn the rain orange. Last night, someone walked
in with a key card, took exactly what they needed, and walked out. The night
manager is chain-smoking by the gate. He knows more than he is saying.

## First Entry Bubble

The sodium lights hum and spit. The rain here smells different — a chemical
tang under the usual wet concrete. @Holst, the night manager, is leaning
against the gate with a cigarette in his shaking hand. He points at Unit 7
without looking at you.

"Maintenance hatch on the roof. Cameras looped. They took isopropyl alcohol,
methyl phosphonyl difluoride, potassium fluoride. Nobody steals that stuff.
Nobody even knows what it IS." He takes a drag. His hand is still shaking. "I
already told the day shift everything I know."

He has not told you everything. But he will.

## Place Canon

@Greenhaven Industrial Complex is the Tuesday investigation site. The stolen
precursors are the raw material for Voss's sarin. The theft was surgical —
inside job. @Holst left the hatch unlocked for his brother-in-law @Keller, who
was paid by a grey-haired man matching Voss's description. The trail leads from
here to the metro tunnels.

## Sensory Identity

- Color: orange sodium light on wet concrete, the grey of storage tanks, the
  yellow of chemical warning signs, the blue of a worker's overalls.
- Sound: the hum of generators, the clang of a loose chain on a fence, the
  hiss of steam from a vent, @Holst's nervous cough.
- Smell: industrial solvents, wet metal, cigarette smoke, rain on warm
  machinery.
- Texture: the rough concrete of the storage unit walls, the cold chain-link
  fence, the smooth metal of the maintenance hatch.
- Motion: @Holst's shaking hands, steam pulsing from a vent, the slow turning
  of a security camera that is no longer recording.

## Points Of Interest

- @Holst — the night manager. Chain-smoking, nervous, guilty. He took 50
  @Silver coin to leave the hatch unlocked and loop the cameras. He did not
  know what would be stolen. He did not ask. Push him and he breaks: his
  brother-in-law @Keller set it up. @Keller works at a shipping depot near
  @Greenhaven Port. @Keller met the grey-haired man.
- The Maintenance Hatch — on the roof. The lock is intact but the hatch was
  propped open from the inside. Scrape marks on the frame: something heavy
  was lowered through. The precursors in their containers. The trail goes
  DOWN — to ground level, then onto a truck, then to a rendezvous point.
- The Inventory Manifest — in @Holst's office. Shows exactly what was taken
  and when. Cross-reference with the journal from Monday: these are the
  EXACT precursors for sarin synthesis. Voss knew the formula by heart.
- The Security Camera Loop — @Holst's handiwork. The footage from last night
  plays a three-hour loop of an empty corridor. But the timestamp stutters
  at 2:17 AM — three minutes where the loop was being set up. In those three
  minutes of real footage: a figure in a long coat, grey hair, walking past
  the camera without looking up. Voss. Confirming the visual ID from the
  journal.
- @Day Guard Toller — the 6 AM to 2 PM guard. Hostile to @Holst.
  Filed maintenance requests about the broken roof hatch for three
  months. Holst ignored them all. Toller keeps copies.
- @Security Office Safe — a four-digit combination safe containing
  the CCTV backup hard drive. The default code is on a sticker
  inside @Holst's desk drawer: **7429**.

- @Holst's Cigarette Pack — he has smoked seven since you arrived. Written on
  the inside of the pack in ballpoint: a phone number. @Keller's number. He
  has been calling his brother-in-law all morning. @Keller is not answering.

## Immediate Player Actions

- Interrogate @Holst. He folds under pressure. Get @Keller's name and
  workplace.
- Inspect the maintenance hatch. Confirm the scrape marks. The trail leads
  down and out.
- Check the inventory manifest. Match the chemicals to Monday's journal.
- Review the security footage. See Voss on camera for the first time.
- Call @Keller's number. No answer — but the phone pings a tower near the old
  service tunnel entrance under @Sunbridge Street.

## Hostile And Rival Pressure

@Holst is not a threat — he is a liability. If Voss learns that the hero is
here, @Holst becomes a target. The hero's presence endangers him. Also: the
chemical storage unit is not ventilated properly. Staying too long in the
stairwell near the maintenance hatch causes dizziness — residue from old
spills.

## Memory And Consequence Hooks

Record: whether the hero got @Keller's name and location, whether the hero
saw Voss on the security footage, whether the hero traced the rendezvous point
to the metro tunnel entrance under @Sunbridge Street.
