PORTRAIT PROMPT — Day Guard Toller (Troll)

A troll security guard, two and a half metres tall, grey-green thick skin. Heavy horns curving back from the temples, worn smooth at the tips. Lower tusks pushing past the lip — left tusk chipped from an old bar fight. Broad shoulders, arms thick as pipes, but posture is careful, deliberate — a giant who has learned to be gentle in small spaces. Security uniform straining at the shoulders, sleeves too short, collar unable to button over the thick troll neck. A heavy flashlight on the belt — one hand touching it. Chewing gum. Deep circles under the eyes from early shift. Expression: vindicated bitterness, the look of someone who warned everyone for three months and was right. Background: an industrial complex at dawn, chain-link fence, sodium lights, rain. Style: Shadowrun-inspired industrial noir — fantasy race grounded in gritty working-class realism. Square 1:1, 2048x2048. No text.
