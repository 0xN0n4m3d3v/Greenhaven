MUSIC PROMPT — Cold Design Theme

A sparse, minimal ambient piece for the city's first confession. Begins with silence and the sound of rain. A single deep piano note — struck and left to decay. Slowly joined by a low synth drone and the distant sound of grinding metal. The feeling of standing on an empty road at night, realizing you are being watched by something too old to care. Tempo: free time, no percussion. Key: D minor. Instruments: felt piano, processed metallic drones, rain, silence. Duration: 2-3 minutes, loop-ready.
