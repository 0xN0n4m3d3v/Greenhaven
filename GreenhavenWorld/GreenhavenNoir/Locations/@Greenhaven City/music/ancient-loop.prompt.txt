MUSIC PROMPT — Ancient Loop Theme

A hypnotic, circular ambient piece. A repeating four-note pattern on a processed piano — the same notes, over and over, with slight degradation each cycle. Underneath: the sound of water dripping in an underground space, the distant grinding of vast gears. The feeling of realizing you have been here before. You will be here again. Tempo: 60 BPM, the loop is the point. Key: A minor. Instruments: felt piano (processed with tape warble), metallic drones, dripping water, gear sounds, very subtle sub-bass. Duration: 3-4 minutes, designed to loop seamlessly — the end IS the beginning.
