MUSIC PROMPT — Crimson Dusk Theme

A darker, warmer ambient piece — the city showing its bleeding skyline. A low cello melody that bends and warps, like heat haze over asphalt. Distorted violin harmonics floating above. The distant sound of something heavy dropping into water — again and again. The mood is not horror — it is the beauty of something wrong. Warm but sick. Tempo: 55 BPM, slow pulse. Key: E minor with microtonal bends. Instruments: warped cello, processed violin, sub-bass pulses, water recordings, distorted chimes. Duration: 3-4 minutes, loop-ready.
