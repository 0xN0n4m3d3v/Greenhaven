MUSIC PROMPT — Greenhaven City Ambient Theme

A dark, slow, atmospheric ambient track for a noir city that never sleeps. Low droning bass — the sound of underground machinery breathing. Layers of rain on metal. Occasional distant electric hum — a neon sign on its last circuit. A single deep cello note held for measures at a time, like the city exhaling. Tempo: 50-60 BPM. Key: minor — D minor or C minor. Instruments: processed cello, deep synth pads, rain field recordings, metallic drones, sub-bass. Mood: ancient, indifferent, vast, melancholic without sentimentality. The city is not sad — it simply IS. Duration: 3-4 minutes, seamless loop.
