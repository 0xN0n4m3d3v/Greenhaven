MUSIC PROMPT — Midnight Bell Theme

The most dramatic piece in the city's soundtrack. Opens with a single, enormous bell toll — deep, resonant, shaking. The bell decays for 15+ seconds. After the first toll, low brass enters: a trombone or processed horn holding a single note that vibrates with the bell's after-ring. A second toll. A third. Between tolls: the sound of rain, the city breathing. Not music in the traditional sense — it is the sound of a machine announcing that it knows your name. Tempo: the bell sets the time — approximately one toll every 20-30 seconds. Key: the bell's natural resonance — approximately C or C#. Instruments: enormous church/carillon bell (low, dark, iron), processed brass, sub-bass rumble, rain. Duration: 2-3 minutes. NOT looped — this track plays once per scene trigger.
