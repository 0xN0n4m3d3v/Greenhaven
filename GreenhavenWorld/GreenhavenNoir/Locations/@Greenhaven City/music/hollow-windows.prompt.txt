MUSIC PROMPT — Hollow Windows Theme

The most intimate piece. Very quiet. A single, close-mic'd piano playing sparse notes — as if someone is touching the keys in an empty room, unsure if they are allowed to play. Long silences between notes. Underneath: the softest possible rain. The occasional distant electric hum — a sign, a wire, a memory of current. The feeling of being alone in a city of millions of windows, and knowing that one of them is watching you — not with malice, but with recognition. Tempo: free, very slow. Key: F minor. Instruments: close-mic'd upright piano, rain, silence, the faintest synth pad at the threshold of hearing. Duration: 3-4 minutes, loop-ready.
