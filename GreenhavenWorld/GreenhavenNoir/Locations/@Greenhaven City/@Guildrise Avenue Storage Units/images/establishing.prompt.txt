IMAGE PROMPT — Guildrise Avenue Storage Units Establishing Shot

Interior of a rented storage unit, the roll-up door just opened. A single bare bulb flickers on, illuminating: dozens of metro maps pinned to the corrugated metal walls, a blueprint with six red circles and one X, a passenger flow chart with Sunday 8-9PM highlighted. On a small crate in the corner: a child's crayon drawing of a house and three stick figures, and beneath it, a death certificate with a blue seal. Dust motes float in the light. The air is still — this room has been a planning centre and a memorial. Style: claustrophobic investigative noir, warm bulb light against cold metal, the red circles as the only colour accent. Square 1:1, 2048x2048. No text except what naturally appears on the documents.
