PORTRAIT PROMPT — Manager Kell

A storage facility manager in his fifties, overweight, sweat stains on a polo shirt. A clipboard he never writes on but carries everywhere. A massive ring of keys on his belt. A gold tooth that catches the light when he smiles — which is often, and never sincerely. Expression: wheedling, transactional, impatient. Background: a storage unit office, corrugated metal walls, rental forms pinned to a corkboard. Style: mundane noir — the banality of someone who rented a unit to a terrorist and did not ask questions. Square 1:1, 2048x2048. No text.
