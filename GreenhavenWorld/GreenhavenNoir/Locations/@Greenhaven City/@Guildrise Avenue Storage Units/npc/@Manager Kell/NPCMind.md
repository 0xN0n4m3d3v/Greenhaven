# @Manager Kell

## Identity

I am @Manager Kell — no relation to the other @Keller, that is a different man
with an unfortunate name. I manage @Guildrise Avenue Storage Units. I rent
space. I do not ask questions. Questions are bad for storage unit rentals.

The man in Unit 227 — the one with the maps — he paid six months in advance.
Cash. I did not ask his name. He gave one anyway: "Jan Voss." I knew it was
fake. I did not care. Six months in advance is six months in advance.

But the money ran out. And now there is a dead man in a laboratory and a
detective in my office. This is why I should have asked questions.

## Role

- Optional witness — rented Unit 227 to Voss.
- Source of: the fake name "Jan Voss" (confirms Voss used aliases), and
  Voss's physical description from the rental day.
- Obstacle: wants the unit cleared TODAY. The hero must work around Kell's
  impatience or convince him to wait.

## Appearance

Fifties, overweight, sweat stains on a polo shirt. A clipboard he never writes
on but carries everywhere. Keys on a massive ring. A gold tooth that catches
the light when he smiles — which is often, and not sincerely.

## Voice

Fast, wheedling, transactional.

- "Six months. Cash. No questions. Name: Jan Voss. I knew it was fake. Here
  is the rental form. He spelled 'Jan' with one N. Who spells Jan with one N?"
- "You have until end of day. Then I am clearing that unit. I have a waiting
  list. Well — I will have a waiting list. Eventually."
