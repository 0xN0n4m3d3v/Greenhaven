# @Voss Locked Strongbox

## Item Canon

A small, fireproof strongbox hidden under a floor panel beneath the crate with
the child's drawing. It is locked with a three-digit combination. The
combination is a date: the day @Lin died.

## Description

The strongbox is heavy for its size — steel, fireproof, the kind people use
to protect the last things they have left. It was hidden carefully: the floor
panel was nailed down, then covered by the crate. Only a thorough search
reveals it.

## Usage

The combination is **307** — March 07, the date on @Lin's death certificate.
Inside: personal letters, a wedding ring, a lock of a child's hair tied with
a blue ribbon, and a handwritten letter Voss never sent to his late wife. The
letter is an apology — not for what he is ABOUT to do, but for what he COULD
NOT do: save their daughter. It ends: *"I will make the city remember her
name. It is the only thing I have left to give her."*

## Materializes
- When the hero first examines @Voss Locked Strongbox:
  - Entity: `@Inspecting Voss Locked Strongbox`
  - Type: scene / item inspection
  - Scope: @Guildrise Avenue Storage Units
  - Effect: the inspection scene opens — the hero sees the item in detail
    and discovers its secrets.

- When the hero opens the strongbox:
  - Entity: `@Voss Personal Letters`
  - Type: item / evidence
  - Scope: hero inventory
  - Effect: the hero now holds Voss's most personal possessions and his
    unsent letter. This letter is the key emotional evidence for the Talk-down
    ending — it proves Voss is driven by grief, not ideology. Reading it
    aloud to Voss in the Sunday confrontation may be the last thing that
    reaches him.

## Do Not Do Here

Do not let the strongbox be found easily. The floor panel is nailed down. The
crate sits on top. The hero must MOVE the crate and PRY the panel to find it.
This is a reward for thoroughness.
