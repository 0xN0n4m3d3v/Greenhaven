Greenhaven Quest
AI-agent narrative game / psychological LitRPG

Subject: Greenhaven: fictional life as state, not chat context

Greenhaven is a playable AI-agent narrative game where memory, relationships,
quests, inventory, scenes, combat, and consequences live in a database-backed
runtime.

The model is not asked to pretend it remembers. The world gives the agent
memory, tools, rules, state, specialists, and a body it can act through.

Why this is investable:

- Product: playable now. Backend, React client, desktop path, SSE events,
  tools, quests, inventory, maps, and specialists already exist.
- Architecture: world database. NPCs, items, locations, quests, scenes, and
  coins share one object backbone.
- Core edge: NPC memory and NPC state mutation. Relationships, wounds,
  promises, status, private notes, and future behavior mutate through play.

Greenhaven sits at the intersection of romantasy, fanfiction, manhwa, LitRPG,
tabletop roleplay, and AI character chat.

For readers:
Slow burn, hidden routes, real memory, visible progression.

For AI platforms:
A serious testbed for durable memory, tool discipline, long-running agents,
and emotionally coherent role reliability.

For investors:
A working product in a new category: a database-first AI-agent narrative RPG,
not a thin chatbot wrapper.

What we want:
Strategic investors, AI platform partners, API credits, model review access,
and technical conversations around long-running agentic fiction.

Competitive frame:
Character.AI, AI Dungeon, NovelAI, RoleForge, Friends & Fables, Inworld, and
Convai each solve adjacent pieces. Greenhaven combines memory, tools, game
state, specialists, and reader-facing play in one product.
