AI-agent narrative game / psychological LitRPG

Fictional life as state, not chat context.

Greenhaven is a playable AI-agent narrative game where memory, relationships, quests, inventory, scenes, combat, and consequences live in a database-backed runtime.

The model is not asked to pretend it remembers. The world gives the agent memory, tools, rules, state, specialists, and a body it can act through.

Why this is investable.

Existing formats let readers watch, choose, chat, or roleplay. Greenhaven lets them leave a persistent mark. It sits at the intersection of romantasy, fanfiction, manhwa, LitRPG, tabletop roleplay, and AI character chat.

For readers: slow burn, hidden routes, real memory, visible progression.
For AI platforms: a serious testbed for durable memory, tool discipline, long-running agents, and emotionally coherent role reliability.
For investors: a working product in a new category: a database-first AI-agent narrative RPG, not a thin chatbot wrapper.

What we want

Strategic investors, AI platform partners, API credits, model review access, and technical conversations around long-running agentic fiction.

Competitive frame

Character.AI, AI Dungeon, NovelAI, RoleForge, Friends & Fables, Inworld, and Convai each solve adjacent pieces. Greenhaven combines memory, tools, game state, specialists, and reader-facing play in one product.

A fantasy world that remembers what you did to it.
